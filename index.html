Smsprospection · AI landing & SMS

Smsprospection – Targeted SMS campaigns with AI-generated landing pages

Big team project where I was responsible for designing and implementing the full UI and contributing to the back-end. The app lets marketers select a city and the type of service they want to promote, then uses APIs and AI to generate a personalized content page for that audience.

The experience is focused on a smooth flow: from segmentation to content generation and campaign setup, with a clean and modern interface that works on desktop and mobile.

UI lead Laravel · PHP · APIs AI content generation
ELECAR · Electric Cars

ELECAR – Exploring sustainable mobility

ELECAR is a showcase website focused on the world of electric cars. It presents EV models, benefits, and the latest industry news in a clear, modern interface designed to highlight sustainable transportation.

The goal was to build a simple, fast and informative experience where visitors can easily understand why electric cars matter and explore different options.

HTML · CSS · JavaScript Responsive layout
GoWilds · Camping & Travel

GoWilds – A static site for outdoor lovers

GoWilds is a static website made for people who love camping and outdoor adventures. It focuses on destinations, tips and gear, with a very clean structure and simple navigation.

SNIT · Veterinary Traceability

SNIT – Traceability platform for animal vaccination

SNIT is a business application designed to help veterinarians and technicians manage vaccination campaigns for cows and sheep with maximum traceability.

The system tracks breeders, animals, vaccination sessions, stock movements and locations, linking everything together on and off the field. I focused heavily on the back-end logic and data model to keep the information consistent and easy to analyze.

Laravel · PHP · MySQL Business logic & data model Traceability & stock management
Certis · Maintenance & Field-Service

Certis – Maintenance & Field-Service Management System

Worked as a full-stack developer on Certis, a complete maintenance management platform built with Laravel, Livewire and MySQL. The system centralizes field-service operations and gives teams a clear view of what needs to be done on site and when.

I implemented preventive and corrective maintenance workflows, task assignment, quality-control steps, user roles and permissions, technician reports and client approval flows. I also integrated OAuth email for OTP and notifications, improved the UI/UX with Tailwind, and handled deployment and configuration on a Linux VPS.

Laravel · Livewire · MySQL OAuth email · OTP & notifications Tailwind UI/UX Linux VPS deployment
